<p>Dear Parents/Guardians</p>
<p>Phase 1 registration is for a Singapore Citizen (SC) or Permanent Resident
(PR) child born between 2 January 2020 and 1 January 2021 (both dates inclusive)
and has an older sibling currently studying in our primary school.</p>
<p>Parents who are registering their child under <strong>Phase 1</strong> are
to register during the Phase 1 registration period<strong> from 9am on 30 June 2026 to 4.30pm on 2 July 2026.</strong> Please
log in to the <strong>P1 Registration Portal</strong> with your <strong>Singpass</strong> to
register during the eligible registration period.</p>
<p>Information on the 2026 P1 Exercise had been published on the <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/p1-registration/how-to-register" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE website</a>.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
