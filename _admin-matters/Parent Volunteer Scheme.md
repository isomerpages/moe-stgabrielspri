**SGPS Parent Volunteer Scheme** (July 2026 to Jun 2027)<br>
[For child to be registered in 2027 and to be admitted to Primary One in 2028]

Applicable only if the child is a Singapore Citizen / Singapore Permanent Resident and is born between 2 January 2021 and 1 January 2022. 

Parents who are interested in applying as Parent Volunteers need to submit the completed [application form](/files/Parent_Volunteer_Application_Form__Register_2027_Admission_2028_.pdf) (via email) to the school at stgps@moe.edu.sg. 

The deadline for submission is **22 May 2026**. (Please note that the application is now closed.)
