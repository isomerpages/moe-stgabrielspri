<p><strong>Angelic Voices, Inspiring Hearts</strong>
</p>
<p>Our <strong>Gabrielite Choir</strong> continued to inspire the school community
in 2025 with their angelic voices, musical excellence, and unwavering dedication
across numerous school celebrations and masses, including the MGEI Dedication
of the School Year Mass, Founder’s Day Mass, Thanksgiving Mass, Lunar New
Year, Reap the Laurels, and National Day Singalong. Supported by talented
piano and violin accompanists, the boys demonstrated commitment, teamwork,
confidence, and perseverance as they honed their craft and led audiences
through song. Through every rehearsal and performance, they embodied the
spirit of future-ready Gabrielites – young leaders who shine with passion,
collaboration, and pride in representing the school community.</p>

<p><strong>Choir Boys Inspire Commuters’ Hearts</strong>
</p>
<p>In collaboration with <strong>SMRT’s Adopt-A-Station (AAS) initiative</strong>,
our Gabrielite Choir brought cheer to commuters with a heartfelt National
Day performance at Serangoon MRT on 7 August 2025. Their spirited renditions
of National Day songs celebrated Singapore’s heritage and inspired a sense
of unity among the public. This platform gave our choir boys an authentic
opportunity to share their musical talents beyond school, nurturing confidence,
teamwork, and pride in the nation. Such experiences prepare our Gabrielites
to be future-ready leaders who contribute meaningfully to the community.</p>
